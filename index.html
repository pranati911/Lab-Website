Introduction



Centre for Microsystems is the central facility at Institute level established in association with Intellisense, Bangalore on January 28, 2020. It focus on the development of academic/research infrastructure to improve the programmes and activities in the area of Microsystems for societal benefits. The objective of this center is to develop, fabricate and characterize new class devices and systems, sensors, energy scavenger, RF- MEMS for their potential applications in every aspect possible. The Microsystems touches every facet of our lives and is expected to bring benefits to numerous application areas including industrial, healthcare and environmental monitoring.

The Undergraduate and Postgraduate students and research scholars are working in the areas of Microsystems and the Centre of Microsystems provide them more professional experiment platform and research internship opportunity . The aim of this centre is to bridge technological gap between industry-academics with hands-on experience and providing with new pedagogical methods to the students pursuing UG and PG programs in engineering thereby improving professional skills.
