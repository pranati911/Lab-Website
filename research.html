Research

























A small intro on research domains

IntelliSense ignited the MEMS industry in the early 1990’s with its IntelliSuite family of CAD tools.

And now Shri Ramdeobaba College of Engineering and Management, in collaboration with Intellisense, has started to work on the same domains.

We together are working on toolset for MEMS layout design, advanced process simulation, FEA, parametric analysis, system simulation, packaging analysis and more.

  2. 2. "Fabrication of Microcantilever based Sensor" (May 2012- May 2013); Investigators: Dr. Rajendra Patrikar, Co-investigator: Prof. (Mrs.) Jayu Kalambe
  3. 3. “Design & Development of High Power MEMS Cantilever Switch” (May 2013 to Aug 2015); Investigators: Dr. R. S. Pande, Co Investigator: Deepak Khushalani (Received Best INUP Project Award)
  4. 4. “Fabrication of microfluidics channel for detection of adulterants in milk” (Dec 2016 to May 2017); Investigators: Dr. Jayu Kalambe, Co Investigator: Sakshi Rana
  5. 5. "Design & Development of MEMS Resonators" (May 2018 to August 2018); Investigators: Dr. Rajesh Pande, Co Investigator: Prassanna Deshpande
